üí° Tips to Stay Safe

  1. Don't answer unknown numbers. Let calls from unrecognized numbers go to voicemail; scammers usually won't leave a message.
  2. Hang up on suspicious calls. If a call is automated or feels off, end it immediately without engaging.
  3. Activate Do Not Disturb (DND). Register your number for DND to block most marketing calls and spam messages.
  4. Use your phone's blocking features. Both iPhone and Android devices allow blocking of unknown or specific numbers.
  5. Install a reputable spam-blocking app. Apps like Truecaller or Hiya can help detect and block scam calls in real time.
  6. Never share personal information. Do not provide sensitive data over the phone unless you are sure about the caller's identity.
  7. Be wary of caller ID. Scammers can 'spoof' numbers to look local‚Äînever trust caller ID alone.
  8. Don't press buttons or respond. Never press numbers or interact during a suspicious call‚Äîit confirms your number is active.
  9. Block and report persistent spam. Manually block numbers and report spam calls to your carrier or authorities.
